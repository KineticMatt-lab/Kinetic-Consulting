Client reporting migration to Aladdin
TCW · Business / Data Analyst
Aladdin Client statements
2024–2025
North America
  • Problem: Legacy SMS reporting, new Aladdin stack, unclear gaps and data breaks.
  • Approach: Compared legacy vs. target reports field‑by‑field, introduced Jira‑based issue capture, defined tolerances and “known differences.”
  • Outcome: A documented path to acceptance with fewer surprises in client‑facing statements.
This is a good fit if you’re mid‑migration to a strategic platform and need someone to own the messy middle—testing, documenting, socializing what’s “okay” vs. what must be fixed.
IBOR transition from Eagle to SimCorp
SWIB · Business / Data Analyst
SimCorp Dimension IBOR Snowflake
2023–2024
North America
  • Problem: IBOR moving from BNY Eagle to SimCorp with complex Markit EDM and Snowflake layers.
  • Approach: Authored requirements, field mappings and reconciliation logic; built Excel / Power BI tooling; structured Jira workflows and meeting cadence.
  • Outcome: A controlled IBOR transition with traceable mappings and fewer downstream surprises.
If you’re staring at multiple data stores and no single source of truth, this type of structure makes the migration survivable.

North America

Securities lending & data modernization
