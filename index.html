Approach
How engagements work

The process is intentionally lightweight and designed to plug into existing program structures.

  • Discovery (1–2 weeks): Clarify scope, stakeholders, systems, and risks. Capture current‑state diagrams and artifacts.
  • Design & alignment: Translate goals into concrete requirements, mappings and controls that both business and technology teams can own.
  • Execution & testing: Support build, data validation, UAT and rollout with clear acceptance criteria and documentation.
  • Handover: Deliver procedures, reference materials and training so your teams are confident after the engagement ends.
